Terracotta Roof Tile Colour Guidance

True terracotta roof tiles are made from natural clay and fired at high temperatures. This process bakes the colour deep into the tile, ensuring it never fades. Because of this natural beauty and durability, we strongly advise against painting natural terracotta.

Our Recommendation

Instead of painting, we recommend a professional wash and necessary repairs to restore their original, vibrant clay finish. Preserving the natural glaze protects the tile's integrity and your home's heritage.
